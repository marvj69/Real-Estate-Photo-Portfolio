The Photographer

Documenting the architecture of the Upper Peninsula

Based in Chassell, Michigan, I specialize in capturing the unique architectural character of homes across the Western Upper Peninsula. From historic Victorian cottages on the Keweenaw shoreline to modern cabins nestled in the Ottawa National Forest, my work emphasizes bright natural light, honest composition, and breathtaking depictions of homes.

With nearly a decade of experience in architectural photography, I understand the angles and features to highlight. Whether you're a Realtor seeking distinctive listing imagery, or a home seller, I bring technical precision and artistic sensitivity to every frame.
