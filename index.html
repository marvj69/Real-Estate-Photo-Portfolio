The Photographer

Documenting the architecture of the Upper Peninsula

Based in Chassell, Michigan, I specialize in capturing the unique architectural character of homes across the Western Upper Peninsula. From historic Victorian cottages on the Keweenaw shoreline to modern cabins nestled in the Ottawa National Forest, my work emphasizes natural light, honest composition, and the subtle interplay between built environments and the rugged Northwoods landscape.

With over a decade of experience in architectural photography, I understand that a home is more than structure—it's a story. Whether you're a homeowner preserving your property's legacy, a realtor seeking distinctive listing imagery, or a designer documenting your craft, I bring technical precision and artistic sensitivity to every frame.
